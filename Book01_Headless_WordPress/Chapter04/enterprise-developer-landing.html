Frequently Asked Questions

Can I read the books out of order? +
Yes! Each book in the series is designed to be standalone, focusing on specific aspects of enterprise WordPress development. While there are references between books, you can start with whichever topic is most relevant to your current projects.
Do I need an Elite Formidable Forms license? +
While many examples use Formidable Forms features, the concepts and patterns taught are applicable to any WordPress development. However, to follow along with all code examples, an Elite license is recommended for access to all features and add-ons.
Are these suitable for agencies or solo developers? +
Absolutely! The series is designed for both scenarios. Agencies will benefit from the scalable architecture patterns and team collaboration techniques, while solo developers will appreciate the comprehensive coverage that helps them deliver enterprise-quality solutions independently.
Will there be video or course versions? +
We're currently developing video companions for select books in the series. Newsletter subscribers will receive early access and special pricing when these become available. Each video course will include hands-on labs and downloadable project files.
